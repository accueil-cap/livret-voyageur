🏠 Accommodation

36 m² apartment in Cap d'Agde located on the 3rd and top floor of a secure residence with private parking.

The beach is about 850 meters away. The accommodation is close to shops, the harbor, amusement park, and main activities.

The accommodation includes:

  • Entrance with bunk beds
  • Living room with sofa bed
  • Fully equipped kitchen
  • Mezzanine bedroom with double bed 140x190
  • Bathroom with toilet
  • Loggia
  • Private parking
